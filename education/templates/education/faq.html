Frequently Asked Questions

What is the purpose of this app?

This application aims to provide accessible first aid education, connect users with medical writers, and offer a quick way to find categorized emergency contacts for various situations.

How do I find emergency contacts?

Navigate to the "Emergency Contacts" page. You can then search for contacts by location or filter by categories like "Hospitals," "Ambulance," or "Poison Control."

Can I contribute articles?

Yes, authenticated users can write and submit articles via the "New Article" button. These articles help enrich our community knowledge base on first aid and safety.
