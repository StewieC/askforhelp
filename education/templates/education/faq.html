Frequently Asked Questions

What is the purpose of this app?

This application aims to provide first aid education, including emergency contacts and resources for handling various emergency scenarios.

How do I find emergency contacts?

Emergency contacts can be searched based on location, and they are categorized in the emergency contacts section of the app.

Can I contribute articles?

Yes, users can contribute first aid articles by submitting them in the 'New Article' section. Admin approval may be required before publishing.
